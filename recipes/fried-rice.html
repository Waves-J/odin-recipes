Fried Rice

Recipe Description

This is a recipe of one of the most recognizable dishes in China, the fried rice. This recipe is simple to follow and uses healthy ingrediants. Without further to do, lets get on with the recipe!

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons of any oil (vegetable or canola)
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 medium cloves garlic
  • 5mL soy sauce
  • 5mL toasted sesame oil
  • salt and white pepper
  • few large eggs
  • 100g frozen peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the cooked rice, a day old preferred, into the wok. Make sure to heat half a tablespoon of oil in the wok until it smokes beforehand.
  2. Add half a spoon of oil again after each rice begins to change color and then add the rest of the ingrediants of onion, carrot, scallions, and garlic into the wok. Toss it around for a minute and add soy sauce and sesame oil and toss it again, finishing with salt and pepper.
  3. Push rice to side of wok and add the remaining half a tablespoon of oil and break egg or eggs into oil, making sure to break it into pieces and toss together.
  4. Add the frozen peas until each peas are thawed and grains of rice is separated.
